Our Mission

We believe that everyone has valuable knowledge to share and something new to learn. SkillSwap is breaking down barriers to education by creating a community where skills are currency and learning is free.

Our Story

SkillSwap was founded in 2024 by a group of lifelong learners who were frustrated with the high cost of education and the untapped potential in their communities. They realized that in any neighborhood, there are people with diverse skills—from coding to cooking, from languages to music—who would love to share their knowledge.

The traditional education model often creates barriers: high tuition costs, rigid schedules, and limited access. We saw an opportunity to create something different—a platform where learning happens through mutual exchange, where teaching and learning go hand in hand, and where community replaces currency.
